COTTAGE FIEND

Deliver cottage cheese from the truck to the fridge. Run over a tub to grab it automatically, then hold the THROW button and release at the right moment to fling it into the fridge. First to 10 deliveries wins the round. Grab parachuting presents for random power-ups (or curses!).

Name yourself mallen to become THE MALLEN — a giant cottage-cheese fiend who pummels delivery folk, devours dropped tubs, and goes into a frenzy. The Mallen wins by eating 10 tubs.
